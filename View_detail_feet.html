Willerby Salsa Eco - BV166

Sleeps up to 6
2 Bedroom

Availability Calendar

This is our deluxe+ grade 2 bedroom caravan (sleeps up to 6 with pull out double in the lounge). This offers wonderfully spacious accommodation in the Bay View area of the park which is fairly central and close to all amenities. This caravan offers a spacious, modern and comfortable environment with many extra features to make it home from home including a fridge, microwave, built-in sound system, double glazing and air heating throughout.

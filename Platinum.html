Platinum

Our Platinum grade caravans are stunning! These caravans are at least 12ft wide and are sited on prime pitches at Craig Tara. They all are fully double glazed and have central heating throughout. They also have decking fitted to enjoy the prime pitch views whether that be a stunning sea-scape or a nature packed golf course view. These caravans offer several extras that make your stay feel more homely and are luxurious and homely. Features vary from van to van so check out each model to select the best to suit your needs. This accommodation will make your holiday a memorable experience for the whole family.

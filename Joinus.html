Interested in joining Deluxe Caravan Holidays?...

By filling in this form we will contact you and discuss options that we can offer for you to get the best out of your holiday home. We offer a wide variety of services from full control of bookings, cleaning, check in/out, laundry etc to simply advertising your caravan for you. Holiday ownership is not cheap and most owners need to offset renting their caravan to help cover or fully pay their site fees. We can help most owners acheive a good balance where you can benefit from your own time away and also cover the bills!

No Monthly Fees

We only charge £100* per year for your listing & Advertisement. This fee can be taken from your first booking, in some cases also!

Quick Money Release

We aim to transfer full payment for the guests holiday stay within 24 Hours of check-in ensuring that the money you get is yours when you need it without waiting months on payouts.

Complete Control

You want 3 weeks in June? no problem! It's your van we are just letting it out for you. If you want the van or you have the van listed in multiple places, tell us and we will remove the date from the system.

Advertisement on OTA's

We can post your van on TripAdvisor, Booking.com, AirBnB and other OTA's under our Account allowing for even greater exposure as you are joining an ever growing community. We manage the account to achieve the highest possible ratings for our brand, you and your caravan.

Cleaning & Check ins

We offer full service with our vans meaning that you don't need to do anything, we will check your guests in, handle entertainment passes, check them out and clean your van every break for only £50 a break. The van is inspected at check out and if any damage has been done this will be repaired and paid for from the security deposit of the guest.

WHAT ARE YOUR FEES?

We charge an annual fee of £100 which covers your joining fee for our website along with receiving your log in details for our owners page. This will allow your caravan to be seen by all visitors to our site and enable booking on the dates you have told us are available. ​We charge a commission fee of 10% per booking taken by us (bookings taken by you and booked through your owners page are completely free of commission). Our final fee is £50 for our Cleaning, Laundry & Check-in/out service. This is not compulsary and you can do this yourself or have someone else do this for you. Checks will be made to ensure that cleaning is done to a satisfactory standard. You can see more about our joining fees at the join us button below!

WHY CHOOSE US?

We are a small family run business and believe that customer service, cleanliness and quality of holiday homes are key to delivering a memorable holiday experience for our guests. ​ We are constantly growing and developing our booking engine to the best experiance we can give for our owners and guests

WHAT DO YOU DO DIFFERENT?

Unlike other private let businesses we offer full control on pricing and we release your funds immediately when your guests check in. It is still your holiday home and you can pick dates you want to keep for yourself or have rented privately. We do not charge per month or annually, we only charge for any booking actually made.

HOW LONG WILL IT TAKE TO GET MY FIRST BOOKING?

We will arrange a meeting with you once you have contacted us or completed the below form, from that point we will begin building your page for the year. We aim to have your page live within a week including your log in details. The meeting will go into depth about all aspects of our business and what we will offer you to ensure that you beleive that we are the correct business for your needs.

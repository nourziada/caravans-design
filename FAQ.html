DO I NEED TO BRING BED LINEN & TOWELS?...

We provide bed linen free of charge with the booking, However we recommend that you bring your own towels as with the swimming pool on site you are very likely to use them.

ARE THE ENTERTAINMENT PASSES INCLUDED?...

The price of the holiday does NOT include entertainment passes. We can however, arrange for passes to be purchased and in the caravan ready for your arrival. This can be selected by logging in to your account and then adding the required amount of extras to the booking.

HOW MUCH IS A CERTAIN DATE/CARAVAN?...

The easiest way to check availability and prices for all our caravans for the full season, Is to click BOOK NOW for a no obligation quote for any of our vans.

DOES THE CARAVAN HAVE GAS CENTRAL HEATING & DOUBLE GLAZING?...

All of our current fleet past Deluxe+ has Double Glazing and most of them include Gas Central Heating. We'd reccomend that you read the description of each van for the specifics of what they offer.

DO YOU ACCEPT PETS?...

Currently the only van in the fleet that can accommodate pets is Carrick Village 14. This does involve a surcharge of £20 per booking. The park is very pet friendly.

DOES THE VAN HAVE PRIVATE PARKING?...

You will need to check the vans description for accurate information about the specifics of each van. Most of our vans do include ONE private space, If you require any additional spaces you MUST use the communial parking provided and no park on the grass around any of our vans

HOW FAR IS THE VAN FROM THE ENTERTAINMENT COMPLEX?...

Check site map here. All of our vans are within a 5 minute walk to the complex.

DO YOU ACCEPT GROUP BOOKINGS?

For group bookings where you would be wanting vans close together o rmultiple vans under the same payment we'd reccomend you call us so that we can judge what type of van would be best for your needs.
